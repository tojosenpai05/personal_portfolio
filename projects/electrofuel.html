Overview

What is it?

A full commercialization blueprint for a premium F&B startup, covering market research and operational design through to org structure and 3-year financial projections.

The Problem

What needed solving?

First-time founders often have a product idea but lack a structured path to commercialization, leaving critical gaps in operations, pricing strategy, and financial planning that undermine viability before launch.

A great product without a commercialization plan doesn't make it to market.
