Overview

What is it?

A digital home for Kelab Sukan Elektronik ZeTa (KSE ZeTa), a Bintulu, Sarawak esports club that plans, runs, and hosts PUBG Mobile, MLBB, and E-Football tournaments for players, schools, and communities across the region.

The Problem

What needed solving?

A club running real, recurring tournaments had no central place to announce events, show who actually runs the club, or give sponsors and partners visibility. Everything lived scattered across social posts and event posters, with no lasting record of the club's history or structure.

A club with a real track record still needs a real home to prove it.

Solution

What was built

A five-page site anchored by a video hero, a scrolling marquee ticker, and an events spotlight grid pulling from the club's real 2025–2026 tournament history. A dedicated games section showcases the three titles ZeTa runs, while a committee spotlight renders the actual org chart and hierarchy. A journey section links out to the club's TikTok and Instagram, and a contact modal routes every inquiry through the club's real email and socials — no personal numbers exposed.
